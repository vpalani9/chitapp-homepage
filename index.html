Welcome to ChitApp

ChitApp is your comprehensive solution for managing all your chit-fund investments in one place. It helps you strategize, monitor, and analyze your investments with ease. With features like estimated calculations, monthly and annual fund insights, and data backup via Google Drive, ChitApp ensures you have all the tools you need to make informed decisions and keep track of your investments effortlessly.

Key Features

Strategize your chit investments well in advance.
Get estimated calculations for better decision-making.
Track monthly fund requirements and yearly closures.
Backup and restore data with Google Drive integration.
Use pending view to prevent oversights.
Track overdue chits easily.
